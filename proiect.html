2020 PROJECTS


What is our long-term goal?

 PETS shelter has 64 dogs and 34 cats. For future, we want to expand our land to be able to house more animals. Besides that, we want to open more shelters in other cities as well. Another amazing thing that we hope is going to happen, is to be able to house 2 dogs in the same cell instead of 4.

Priorities


 -Each dog to have its own shelter.

 -To build a place only for cats.

 -Playground.

 -To have our own clinic for the animals in need.

 -Each donation is appreciated!

Monthly outgoings:


 – Cleaning: 250 euros/ $325;

 – Food: 2.400 euros/$3.200;

 – Constantly fixing and upgrading the shelters;

 – Clinic: Vaccines, disinfestation, sterilization, medical support for all the sick dogs/dog we find. Since we don't have a clinic here we need support from other private clinic.
