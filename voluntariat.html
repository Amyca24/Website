VOLUNTEERING

Animals are part of our lives since forever. The human's best friend:A dog, it can fill a heart with so much joy and it can bring so much happiness even in the hardest moments. At the same time, humans are giving dogs the same happiness. Those are the lucky dogs.

There are also some unlucky dogs, that have been abandoned by their owners and ended up in the shelter. They live a miserable life on the streets, being beaten, scared for their life, starving in very cold or very hot weather with only one wish: To be loved. Here we have two wishes for our dogs: To be adopted and loved. If you can't adopt a dog, but still want to help and be close to our friendly animals, there is something you can do! Volunteer or donate money/food or toys for the animals. Anything helps.

Either you save a dog from the street that is lost and you bring it back home, either you volunteer to a shelter, your action is admirable and amazing.For starting to volunteer at PETS shelter, send us a letter HERE.

You can donate toys or pet supplies such as water bowls, food or leashes.

We need volunteers for the following:

-Taking pictures of the pets for our website

-Transport animals with their car (if there's possible).

-Advertise (flyers, posters,shares on social media)

-Check on already adopted animals

-Papers (eg:Animal cruelty->PETA)

-Fixing around

-Cleaning

Help the animals!
